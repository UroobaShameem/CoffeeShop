Products

Cappucino

A cappuccino is an espresso-based coffee drink that originated in Italy and is prepared with steamed milk foam.

Expresso

Espresso is a coffee-brewing method, in which a small amount of nearly boiling water is forced under pressure through finely-ground coffee beans.

Latte

A latte or caffè latte is a milk coffee that boasts a silky layer of foam as a real highlight to the drink. A true latte will be made up of one or two shots of espresso.

Frappe

A frappé coffee is a Greek iced coffee drink made from instant coffee, water, sugar, and milk The frappé was invented through experimentation by Dimitris Vakondios.

Mocha

A caffè mocha also called mocaccino is a chocolate-flavoured warm beverage that is a variant of a caffè latte commonly served in a glass rather than a mug.

Americano

Americano is a type of coffee drink prepared by diluting an espresso with hot water, giving it a similar strength to, but different flavor from, traditionally brewed coffee.
